What is Integrated Pest Management (IPM)?

  • Biological controls: Natural predators like ladybugs for aphids
  • Organic remedies: Neem oil, chili-garlic spray, soap solutions
  • Cultural methods: Crop rotation, intercropping, sanitation
  • Mechanical control: Hand-picking pests, traps, barriers
  • Chemical control: Use pesticides only when absolutely needed
